Producer-Consumer Process

Step 1 of 6
Step 1: Producer Starts
The producer thread begins execution and prepares to create a new item.
Step 2: Item Produced
The producer creates a new item and prepares to add it to the buffer.
Step 3: Check Buffer Space
The producer checks if there is space in the buffer. If full, it waits.
Step 4: Item Added to Buffer
The item is placed into the shared buffer.
Step 5: Consumer Retrieves Item
The consumer checks the buffer, finds an item, and removes it.
Step 6: Item Consumed
The consumer processes the item. The cycle can now repeat.
